The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a yurt?

Our luxury yurts are permanent structures four feet off the ground. Each yurt is fully enclosed within canvas walls, a wooden floor , and a roof dome that can be opened

How are yurts furnished?

Each yurt is furnished with a queen size bed with down quilt and gas fired stove. Your luxury camping experience includes electricty and a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.

What should I bring?

Most guests pack comfortable walking shoes and plann to dress for changing weather with light layers of clothing. It is also helpful to bring a flashlight and a sense of adventure
